Chase, we want freedom

Chase Bank is yanking away some consumer rights for credit card holders, but we can stop them.

Basically, they're changing the contract so that we can't sue them in open court, or participate in class action lawsuits, if they mistreat us as customers. Instead, we must use arbitration, and sue individually.
